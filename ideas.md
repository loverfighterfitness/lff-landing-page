## Idea 1: "Raw Iron" — Industrial Strength Aesthetic

<text>

**Design Movement:** Industrial Brutalism meets Athletic Performance

**Core Principles:**
1. Raw, unpolished textures that evoke the grit of a real gym
2. High-contrast typography that commands attention
3. Vertical rhythm and stacked sections that feel like weight plates on a bar
4. Warm earth tones grounded in the LFF brown/cream palette

**Color Philosophy:** The brown (#54412F) acts as the anchor — like worn leather and iron rust. Cream (#EAE6D2) is the breathing space, like chalk dust in the air. Accents of a warm amber/gold (#C4963A) for CTAs to signal "action" and "premium." Deep charcoal (#2A2118) for text weight.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed vertical scroll with alternating dark/light bands. Sections are stacked with hard horizontal dividers (no curves). Content is left-aligned with generous negative space on the right, creating a magazine editorial feel. Asymmetric two-column layouts for testimonials and packages.

**Signature Elements:**
- Diagonal clip-path cuts between sections (like the angle of a barbell)
- Grainy texture overlay on hero and dark sections
- Bold knockout text (text clipped to images)

**Interaction Philosophy:** Heavy, deliberate interactions. Buttons feel weighty with thick borders and solid fills. Hover states shift colour with a slight scale-up. Scroll-triggered reveals are slow and purposeful — no bouncy animations.

**Animation:** Sections fade-and-slide-up on scroll with staggered delays. CTA buttons have a subtle pulse on idle. Package cards lift with a shadow on hover. The VSL section has a cinematic fade-in.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Oswald (bold, condensed, uppercase for headings) — gym poster energy
- Body: Source Sans 3 (clean, readable, modern)
- Accent: Oswald Light for subheadings and labels

</text>

## Idea 2: "Warm Minimalism" — Premium Coaching Studio

<text>

**Design Movement:** Scandinavian Warm Minimalism meets Luxury Fitness

**Core Principles:**
1. Generous whitespace that signals premium positioning
2. Warm, earthy palette that feels inviting, not intimidating
3. Subtle sophistication — every element earns its place
4. Photography-forward layout that builds trust through real imagery

**Color Philosophy:** Cream (#EAE6D2) dominates as the canvas — warm, not sterile. Brown (#54412F) is used sparingly for anchoring elements (nav, footer, key headings). A muted terracotta (#A0705A) bridges the two as a mid-tone for borders, icons, and secondary text. CTAs use a rich espresso (#3A2A1A) with cream text for maximum contrast without introducing a foreign colour.

**Layout Paradigm:** Single-column centered flow with wide max-width (1100px). Sections breathe with 120px+ vertical padding. Content blocks alternate between full-width imagery and contained text. Testimonials in a horizontal scroll carousel. Package cards in a clean 3-column grid that collapses gracefully on mobile.

**Signature Elements:**
- Soft rounded image frames with a thin brown border
- Subtle cream-to-white gradient backgrounds between sections
- Thin horizontal rules as section dividers (not decorative — functional)

**Interaction Philosophy:** Light, refined interactions. Buttons have gentle background transitions. Cards have a soft elevation change on hover. Everything feels smooth and intentional — like walking into a well-designed studio.

**Animation:** Fade-in on scroll with short duration (300ms). Staggered card reveals. Smooth parallax on the hero image. Testimonial carousel auto-advances with a gentle slide.

**Typography System:**
- Display: DM Serif Display (elegant serif for headings) — premium, trustworthy
- Body: DM Sans (geometric sans-serif, clean and modern)
- Accent: DM Sans Medium for labels, nav items, and button text

</text>

## Idea 3: "Fighter's Edge" — Bold Athletic Brand

<text>

**Design Movement:** Modern Athletic Branding meets Editorial Design

**Core Principles:**
1. Bold, unapologetic typography that owns the screen
2. Dynamic layouts that create visual energy and movement
3. Dark-mode dominant with cream as the accent — flipping the expected hierarchy
4. Strong brand identity that feels like a real fitness brand, not a template

**Color Philosophy:** Dark brown/near-black (#2A1F15) as the primary background — moody, powerful, gym-at-5am energy. Cream (#EAE6D2) becomes the hero accent colour — text, borders, highlights. The original brown (#54412F) serves as a mid-ground for cards and secondary surfaces. A warm gold (#D4A853) for CTAs and key action moments — it says "premium" and "go."

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric grid with overlapping elements. Hero section uses a split layout — bold text on one side, imagery bleeding off the edge on the other. Sections use varied column structures (60/40, 70/30) to create visual rhythm. Full-bleed dark backgrounds with contained cream content blocks create depth.

**Signature Elements:**
- Oversized display typography that breaks the grid (text extending beyond containers)
- Cream-outlined cards on dark backgrounds (no fills — just borders)
- A persistent floating CTA button that follows the user

**Interaction Philosophy:** Energetic but controlled. Hover states are immediate and bold (colour inversions, underline animations). Scroll interactions feel athletic — elements slide in from the sides with momentum. The page has forward energy, always pushing toward the CTA.

**Animation:** Elements enter from left/right on scroll (not just fade-up). Heading text animates letter-by-letter on first view. Package cards have a border-glow effect on hover. The floating CTA has a subtle breathing animation.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Bebas Neue (tall, condensed, all-caps) — pure athletic energy
- Body: Inter Tight or Outfit (modern, slightly condensed sans-serif for readability)
- Accent: Bebas Neue at lighter weight for subheadings and labels

</text>
